# Surokkha AI — Demo Video Script (≤ 3 minutes)

> **Goal:** Show the product *working*, not a sales pitch. Screen-record the live web app, narrate in Bangla, burn in / upload the English subtitles (`demo-subtitles.srt`). Upload to YouTube as **Public or Unlisted**.

**Total run time: ~2:45.** Keep it under 3:00 — judges stop watching at 3:00.

---

## Before you record (checklist)

- [ ] App deployed and open at your live Vercel URL (or `localhost:3000`).
- [ ] Gemini key working (so the result shows **"Engine: Rules + AI (Gemini)"**).
- [ ] Browser zoom 100–110%, close other tabs, hide bookmarks bar.
- [ ] Use a screen recorder: **OBS Studio** (free), or Loom, or even phone screen-record if you open the site on a phone.
- [ ] Record at 1080p. Speak slowly and clearly in Bangla.
- [ ] Practice once so each demo lands in time.

## Recording tips

- **One take is fine.** Small stumbles are okay — judges want to see the product work, not polish.
- If a result takes a second to load, that's good — it shows it's real (calling a live AI).
- Keep your cursor movements slow and deliberate.
- Make sure the **red alert, the reasons, and the green safe result** are all clearly visible — those three moments are the whole demo.

## Uploading to YouTube (so judges can watch)

1. Go to <https://youtube.com> → **Create → Upload video**.
2. Set visibility to **Unlisted** (or Public) — **never Private**, judges can't open Private.
3. Add the subtitles: on the video → **Subtitles → Add → English → Upload file → select `demo-subtitles.srt`**.
   (Or upload a version with subtitles already burned in.)
4. Copy the link — that's your **Demo video** submission.
