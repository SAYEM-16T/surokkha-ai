**Subject:** Surokkha AI — a working demo + your advice before tomorrow's AIVA deadline

Hi [Mentor's name],

Hope you're doing well. Ahead of tomorrow's AIVA submission, I've built a **working prototype** of my idea — **Surokkha AI** — and I'd really value five minutes of your feedback.

**What it is**
Surokkha AI is a personal-safety web app that detects scams and manipulation inside chat/SMS messages — and explains *why* — in **Bangla, Banglish, and English**. Think of it as Truecaller, but for the *content* of a conversation instead of the caller.

**What it does (you can try it live)**
- Paste any message (or tap a sample) → it returns a verdict — **Safe / Be careful / Scam** — with a risk score and the exact reasons.
- Detects **bKash/Nagad OTP fraud, lottery scams, romance/marriage ("I'll take you abroad") scams, and grooming/manipulation**.
- Understands all three: **Bangla script, Banglish (romanized), and English** — with a one-tap **Bangla⇄English interface toggle**.
- **Privacy-first** — messages aren't stored anywhere.

**How I built it**
A hybrid detection engine in three layers: (1) a fast rule layer for the unmistakable scams (OTP requests, lottery, fake transactions), (2) an AI language model that catches subtle, paraphrased scams across the three languages, and (3) an explanation layer that surfaces the reasons so the user learns to spot scams.
